Long Scrolling Menu
  • When a long sub-menu has more items than can be displayed in a page, you can view the hidden items by moving the mouse to the up arrow at the top of the sub-menu or to the down arrow at the bottom of the sub-menu.
  • This demo works incorrect because of parent IFRAME size, but it works OK on separate page.
  • See an example below under Products > Menu.
